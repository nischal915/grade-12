Computer Science - Grade 12

Course Description

Welcome to Computer Science Grade 12! This course builds upon fundamental concepts learned in previous years and introduces advanced topics in programming, data structures, algorithms, and computer systems. Students will develop problem-solving skills and gain practical experience through hands-on projects and coding exercises.

The course covers seven comprehensive chapters that explore various aspects of computer science, preparing students for higher education and careers in technology.
